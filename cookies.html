Cookie Policy

Understand how we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and how you can control your cookie preferences.

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

How Cookies Work

Cookies are not programs and cannot contain viruses or malware. They are simple text files that help websites remember your actions and preferences over time. When you visit our site, we may place cookies on your device to enhance functionality and analyze site usage.

Cookie Safety

Cookies are completely safe and cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. They are simple text files that can only be read by the website that created them.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies for various purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Essential
Strictly Necessary Website functionality, security, and basic operations Session Yes
Preferences Remember your settings, language preferences, and customized features 1 Year No
Analytics Understand how visitors use our site to improve user experience 2 Years No
Marketing Deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness 90 Days No

Session vs Persistent Cookies

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted

3. Why We Use Cookies

Cookies help us provide you with a better, more personalized experience on our website:

Personalization

Remember your preferences, language settings, and customized features to enhance your browsing experience.

Analytics

Understand how visitors interact with our website to improve content, navigation, and user experience.

Functionality

Enable essential features like contact forms, shopping carts, and user authentication systems.

Security

Protect your data and prevent fraudulent activities through secure authentication methods.

Legal Basis

We only use non-essential cookies with your explicit consent. Essential cookies that are necessary for the website to function do not require consent under data protection laws.

4. Managing Cookie Preferences

You have full control over which cookies you accept. Here's how you can manage your preferences:

Cookie Settings

Toggle the switches below to enable or disable different types of cookies:

Strictly Necessary Cookies Required for basic website functionality. Cannot be disabled.
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences for future visits
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our website
Marketing Cookies Used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaigns

Browser Cookie Settings

You can also manage cookies through your web browser settings. Here's how to access these settings in popular browsers:

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data

Mozilla Firefox

Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Apple Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and data stored

Deleting Cookies

You can delete existing cookies from your device at any time through your browser settings. Please note that deleting cookies may reset your preferences and affect website functionality.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that also place cookies on your device:

Analytics Services

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. These services use cookies to collect information about your visit, including:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Referring websites and search terms
  • Device type and browser information
  • General geographic location

Social Media Cookies

We integrate with social media platforms that may set cookies when you interact with social sharing buttons or embedded content.

Third-Party Policies

Please note that third-party cookies are subject to the privacy policies of those respective companies. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information about how they handle your data.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our services. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • Notifications on our website
  • Email communications to registered users
  • Updated timestamps on this policy page
