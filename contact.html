Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical website project take?

Most website projects take 2-4 weeks from initial consultation to launch. Complex projects with custom features may take 4-6 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance plans starting from KSh 3,000/month. This includes security updates, backups, technical support, and performance optimization.

Can you work with my existing website or system?

Absolutely! We specialize in website redesigns, system upgrades, and integration with existing platforms. We'll assess your current setup and recommend the best approach.

What's included in your free consultation?

Our free consultation includes project assessment, requirement analysis, initial recommendations, and a preliminary quote. It's a no-obligation discussion to understand your needs and how we can help.
